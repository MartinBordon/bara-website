Applied academic research and advisory for public policy development.

BARA is an independent academic research and advisory practice. Its scope is intentionally broad, covering applied economics and public policy topics, with an emphasis on clarity, methodological consistency, and usefulness for decision-making.

About

Bordón Academic Research Advisory (BARA) is an independent platform created to provide academically grounded research and advisory services. It is designed for institutions and organizations that require technically robust analysis with clear policy implications.

Core fields

Economic analysis · Public policy · Applied research

Founder

Founded by Martín Bordón, PhD researcher and consultant in energy economics and public policy. His work focuses on energy economics, energy poverty, rebound effects, and electricity system analysis.

Published in peer-reviewed journals including Energy Efficiency and Energy for Sustainable Development.

BARA serves as a platform for independent research and advisory work, collaborating with researchers and professionals across complementary fields when required.
