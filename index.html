About

Bordón Academic Research Advisory (BARA) is an independent platform created to provide academically grounded research and advisory services. It is designed for institutions and organizations that require technically robust analysis with clear policy implications.

The practice is designed to support a wide range of topics in economics and public policy, depending on the needs of each project. It combines empirical methods, theoretical grounding, and institutional understanding, collaborating with researchers and professionals across complementary fields when required.

Founder

Founded by Martín Bordón, PhD researcher and consultant in energy economics and public policy. His work focuses on energy economics, energy poverty, rebound effects, and electricity system analysis.

Published in peer-reviewed journals including Energy Efficiency and Energy for Sustainable Development. He has also worked on policy-oriented projects with public institutions and international organizations.

BARA serves as a platform for independent research and advisory work, drawing on a broader network of collaborators across different fields of economics depending on the requirements of each project.
